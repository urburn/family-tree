The McGinley Clan

The most comprehensive online resource dedicated to the McGinley name and its many variants — McGinlay, Ginley, MacGinley, Ginnell, and others. Covers history, heraldry, geography, and a worldwide registry of McGinley families. An essential first stop for any McGinley researcher.
