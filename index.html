The Four Family Lines

Each of the four lines that converge in the McGinley family carried remarkable stories.

🍀

McGinley — Ireland

Great Great Grandfather Maurice McGinley was born at Dundalk, near Dublin, Ireland, and moved his family to Lancashire, England. His son Francis married Mary Agnes Wynne in 1901 at Widnes. Their son Maurice was born in 1902 — he would become the patriarch of the Canadian McGinley family.

⚓

Stephen — Scotland

The Stephen family of Peterhead were shipbuilders, fish curers, and mariners. Great Great Great Grandfather George Stephen served with Lord Nelson and lost an eye at the Battle of Trafalgar (1805). His descendants spread to Canada, Australia, and South Africa.

⛵

Tall — England & Scotland

Great Grandfather William Tall was born in 1866 in Lancaster, England, and married Henrietta Stephen of Peterhead in 1895. Their daughter Henrietta Teresa Tall, born in Lerwick, Shetland Islands in 1900, married Maurice McGinley in Vancouver in 1927.

⚔️

West — Ireland & Scotland

The West family connects to Vice Admiral Temple West, honoured with a monument in Westminster Abbey (1761). His line passed through Ireland and Scotland before connecting to the Stephen family through Alice West, who married Robert Stephen in 1868.

Family Timeline

1727
Temple West enters the Royal Navy aboard HMS Revenge. He would rise to Vice Admiral and be commemorated in Westminster Abbey.
1805
George Stephen serves with Lord Nelson at the Battle of Trafalgar; loses an eye in the engagement.
1828
James Stephen marries Ann Cordiner in Scotland. Their six children — all shipbuilders and fish curers in Peterhead — include Robert Stephen (b. 1841).
1868
Robert Stephen marries Alicia (Alice) Margaret West in Muchalls, Scotland. They will have 12 children.
1895
William Tall marries Henrietta (Ettie) Stephen in Peterhead, Scotland.
1900
Henrietta Teresa Tall born in Lerwick, Shetland Islands, Scotland — the future Grandmother McGinley.
1901
Francis McGinley marries Mary Agnes Wynne at St. Marie's R.C. Church, Widnes, Lancashire, England.
1902
Maurice McGinley born at Widnes, Lancashire, England. Robert Stephen emigrates to Northwest Territories, Canada, becoming a pioneer rancher (Bar S Ranch).
1927
Maurice McGinley marries Henrietta Teresa Tall on August 16th at Our Lady of Perpetual Help parish, Vancouver, B.C.
1929–1931
Francis George McGinley born April 25, 1929; Maurice Gerrard McGinley born September 19, 1931. Both in Vancouver, B.C.
1956–1961
Francis George (lawyer) marries Barbara Flaten in Vancouver. Maurice Gerrard (Gerry, university executive) marries Catherine Carroll McNamara in Vancouver.
1956–1968
Next generation born across Canada: Sharon Ann, William Mark, Robert (children of Francis George); Maurice Joseph, Megan Teresa, Michael Patrick, Sarah Anne (children of Gerry).
